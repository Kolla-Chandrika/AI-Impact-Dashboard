# AI Impact on Employment 2030 – Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview

The **AI Impact on Employment 2030 Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze how Artificial Intelligence (AI) may influence employment trends across various industries and job roles by the year 2030.

This dashboard provides insights into AI replacement risk, future job demand, required skills, and employee upskilling needs. The goal is to help organizations, decision-makers, and professionals understand workforce transformation and prepare for the future job market.

---

## Project Description

As AI adoption continues to grow, organizations must understand its impact on employment, workforce planning, and skill development. This dashboard explores industry-level and job-level data to identify sectors most vulnerable to automation, roles with high future demand, and critical skills required to remain competitive in an AI-driven economy.

The dashboard enables users to analyze employment trends, compare industries, evaluate future demand scores, and identify employees who may require additional training or upskilling.

## Business Problem Statement

The rapid advancement of Artificial Intelligence is transforming the modern workplace. Organizations face challenges in understanding which jobs are at risk of automation, which skills will remain valuable, and how to prepare employees for future workforce demands.

Without data-driven insights, businesses may struggle to develop effective workforce strategies and upskilling programs. This dashboard helps address these challenges by providing a comprehensive view of AI's impact on employment and future workforce requirements.

## Key Insights

* AI is expected to significantly reshape employment patterns across industries.
* Certain industries face higher automation and replacement risks than others.
* Technical, analytical, and AI-related skills are becoming increasingly valuable.
* Continuous learning and workforce upskilling are critical for long-term career sustainability.
